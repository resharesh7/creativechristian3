About Mrs. Tiphany Richardson

Mrs. Tiphany Richardson is the founder and director of Creative Christian Learning Center. With years of experience in early childhood education and a heart for faith-based learning, Mrs. Richardson focuses on building confident, curious learners who are prepared for life and leadership.

Her approach combines play-based learning, strong academic foundations, and character development rooted in Christian values.
